About Me.


Hi, my name is Mia Bae! I am coming from an entirely different background as a Registered Nurse. Although I ended up in the medical field, I had an interest in coding ever since I was in high school. I got my first taste of it when I created websites using HTML and CSS through web hosts such as Angelfire.com and Geocities.com. After years of working in healthcare, I realized that I still had a passion for coding and decided to go back to school to learn more. I have no regrets in making the career change.

I attended University of Maryland University College (UMUC), where I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Software Development and Security in May of 2020. I am energetic, confident, ambitious, and personable. I am a fast learner, and this is where my passion lies. I have experience with Java, Python, and C++ through UMUC, as well as HTML and CSS through personal experience. I am eager to learn new things and would like to get more hands-on experience to expand my skills in full-stack development.
